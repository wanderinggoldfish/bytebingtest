About ByteSno

ByteSno combines the authentic flavors of Korean bingsu with cutting-edge robotics. Our unmanned kiosks operate 24/7, so you can enjoy premium shaved ice desserts anytime—no staff needed. Fast, hygienic, and irresistibly Instagrammable.

From classic Injeolmi to local-inspired Milo Dinosaur, our menu is curated for dessert lovers. With zero wait and consistent quality, ByteSno elevates your sweet treat experience in Singapore.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ByteSno food safe?

Yes. All machines self-sanitize and ingredients are stored under SFA-compliant conditions.

How do refunds work?

If an order fails, you will receive an instant digital refund or a redeemable coupon.

Are there vegan options?

Yes. We offer plant-based milk and vegan toppings—just select the vegan label.

Franchise opportunities will open after our pilot phase. Contact us to express interest.
